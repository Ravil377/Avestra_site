What we do

End-to-end services

We configure our business around our customers’ needs. Our services add value at every stage of the supply chain

  • Purchase
    We have long-term relationships with leading petrochemical producers and refining companies. We use hedging, financial tools and logistical solutions to secure stable supplies
  • Store
    We store products in terminals of the largest ports such as Wibax (Finland), Solventas (Turkey), Safiport (Turkey) and Ak-tas (Turkey)
  • Drumming
    We work with any volumes and businesses of any size, we pack products in metal barrels and ship them in batches of the sizes that customers need
  • Deliver
    We trade on a flexible basis, delivering the petrochemicals to the point that the client needs. Petrochemicals are delivered all over the world by sea, by rail, by trucks, including door-to-door
  • Financing
    Our business model prioritises resilience and prudent risk management. We have the financial capabilities and expertise to operate effectively in volatile markets

Global reach and scale

We apply consistently high standards across the Group, and use our global presence, market knowledge and logistics capabilities to maintain the high level of trade

Investments

Manzhouli Far East Gases Co. Ltd

The Manzhouli Far East Gas terminal is located at the Zabaikalsk - Manchuria border railway crossing and is currently the only operating channel for large-scale LPG export from the Russian Federation to China. LPG supplies began in August 2019. The total capacity of the terminal by the end of 2022 will reach 3 (three) million tons per year

First stage capacity 1.0 million tons of LPG per year:
  • 10 transit spherical tanks, 2000 m3 each
  • 2 raliway lines (1520 and 1435 mm) with a total length of 5.8 km
  • Special ramp 840 meters long for 117 places for unloading
  • 30 places for automatic loading of vehicles
Second stage capacity 2.0 million tons of LPG per year:
  • 12 transit spherical tanks, 2000m3 each
  • 2 additional railway lines with a wide track of 1520 mm
  • Unloading platform for 140 tank wagons
  • 50 sphear tanks of 4000 m3 each for simultaneous storage of more than 100 thousand tons of LPG
  • Construction start date - Q3 2021
  • Completion date - Q4 2022
